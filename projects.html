Lady Lane Market

I was commissioned by Tower Hamlets council to build a WordPress website for the new women-centered street market, Lady Lane Market.

Among other things, the website hosts information about the market, the women who work there and a MailChimp newsletter. It also hosts blog-style interviews with the traders, updated regularly.
